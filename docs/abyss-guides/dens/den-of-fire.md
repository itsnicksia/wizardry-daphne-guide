# Den of Fire

Requirements:  The Den of Fire is the second den available to players. It appears after unlocking a new Right Hand of Reversal ability on the fourth level of the Second Abyss, and after talking with Arna in the Royal Capital Adventurer's Guild it can be accessed from the Edge of Town.
