??? map "Enshadowed Well of Life - Zone 3"

    ??? warning "Potential time-savings"
        - Both of the "Secret Path Keeper Vanquished" corrosion sentries can be registered on this route as they are directly south of the Zone 2 Fountain
        - This approach does require defeating the Zone 3 Necrocore boss again, so make sure you come prepared 

    ??? note "Location"
        <div align="center">
        ![](./img/corrosion-loc/loc-corr-2-enshadowed-well-of-life.jpg)
        </div>

    ??? note "Golem Directions"
        <div align="center">
        ![](./img/corrosion-golem/golem-corr-2-enshadowed-well-of-life.jpg)
        </div>

        - Push the golem to the left to block the corrosion sentry's field of view 

    ??? note "Registration: Spotted"
        - Should have been automatically "Spotted" on first run

    ??? note "Registration: Not spotted"
        - Begin at "Secret Passage Minor Harken" (Zone 1 Harken) and go down the stairs to the south
        - Follow the the main path to the upper right-hand corner of Zone 2 and drop down the hole to Zone 3
        - Push the golem to the left to block the corrosion sentry's field of view ("Not spotted" flagged)
        - End at "Enshadowed Well of Life" (Zone 2 Fountain) to register
        - To exit you can use a Hook of Harken, defeat Necrocore again on Zone 3, or take the route back to 
        
    ??? note "Cursed Wheel Entry"

        </div>
