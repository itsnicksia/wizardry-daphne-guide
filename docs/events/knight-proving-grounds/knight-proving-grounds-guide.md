# Knight Proving Grounds

!!! item "This event is permanently available!"

## Guide:

There is no introduction to this event. Simply start the event by heading to the Royal Capital and accepting Beginner Knight Trial in the Featured tab. This will mark the location on your map.

You can obtain up to 600 Seals of the Knight by completing the trials up the Advanced. Specifically, 100 for Beginner, 150 for Intermediate, and 350 for Advanced. There are no missions for this event in particular.

There are several map variations, and are random upon entering. It is not necessary to explore the whole map, you simply need to make it to the end, defeat a certain number of enemies, and defeat the floor boss. Keep in mind, it only counts as a clear upon talking to the masked knight at the end.

??? note "Tips"
    - Remember to carry potions on all three characters.
    - Carry a healing staff switch so you can use healing spells from the MC and/or other characters who have access to dios/madios. 

??? danger "Beginner Boss Fight Tips"
    - This fight consists of three Hobgoblins in a row.

??? danger "Intermediate Boss Fight Tips"
    - This fight consists of two Lizardman Champions in a row.

??? danger "Advanced Boss Fight Tips"
    - This fight consists of three Minotaurs in a row. The middle one is very resistant to physical damage.

??? note "Master Knight's Ring"
    ![](img/knight-ring.jpg)
    - The Knight's Ring is always 4* Red and has fixed stats when using a FAS. The values can be rerolled but not the type of stat lines.
