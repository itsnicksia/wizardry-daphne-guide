    Well of the Mind and Guiding Lights

    Well of the Mind

    The Well of the Mind is a unique main character customization element accessible from the Ruins where you can unlock classes, skills, spells, and stat increases using "Mind Pearls".

    You start with 4 Mind Pearls. More are obtained by using "Guiding Lights" that can be collected through progressing in the main story. Guiding lights are used by unlocking a color-matching clouded area of the Well after activating a node adjacent to that clouded area. This will open those nodes for use and give you an additional 1-4 Mind Pearls.

    Node layout and Mind Pearl costs are noted on the node map below, with node border colors matching the Guiding Light needed for unlocking that area. For the meaning of each node, see the Acronym/Node label description table.

    Work In Progress

    Warning

    The Well can be reset every 24 hours for free. You can reset it sooner than that for 100 green or purple Gems of Org. The skill, spell, proficiency, and class nodes are always in the same location. However, the stat and type change nodes reset randomly. For example, it is possible to have 3 Surety nodes on one reset and just 1 Surety node on another reset.

    Guiding Lights

    Guiding Light Bonus Mind Pearls Method to Obtain
    STR 1 4 Abyss 1: Obtained after unlocking the Cursed Wheel
    STR 2 3 Abyss 4: Learn the first reversal power during the chase sequence in Route 5
    VIT 1 4 Abyss 1: Complete the Beginning Abyss and save the King
    VIT 2 1 Abyss 3: Activate the Golem before the boss fight in Guarda Fortress Abyss
    PIE 4 Abyss 2: Beat Melgina for the first time, regardless of missing person request taken
    IQ 1 4 Abyss 2: Save the missing person, including speaking to him on deck, prior to killing either Melgina or Octo for the second time. This must now be done on your second or later time defeating either Melgina or Octo. It cannot be obtained on your first attempt anymore
    IQ 2 3 Abyss 4: Beat the Greater Warped One a second time (requires fixing the cabin)
    SPEED 4 Abyss 2: 3rd District - Iron Gate Street. Complete the inspection on Guane territory (first time on any path)
    DEX 1 4 Abyss 2: 4th District - Boulevard. After solving the drowning water god statue trap, go back to town and something will have changed in the Well of the Mind
    DEX 2 1 Abyss 3: Get the "Good" Ending in Guarda Fortress Abyss
    SHADOW 1 Abyss 3: Complete the 2nd Part of the Plot of the Sand Shadow Quest
    HONOR 3 Abyss 4: Complete the 2nd Part of the Vendetta on the Island of the Wailing Oni Quest
    LUCK 1 1 Abyss 1: Get the perfect ending (+Albano quest) in Beginning Abyss
    LUCK 2 1 Abyss 2: Save Bonnie and Clyde
    LUCK 3 1 Abyss 2: Get all three best endings on Viscount Guane request
    LUCK 4 1 Abyss 2: Defeat Octanarus
    LUCK 5 1 Abyss 2: Clear all 3 main Port Town requests
    LUCK 6 1 Abyss 2: Clear the Monster Soup quest for Pickerel
    LUCK 7 1 Abyss 3: Stop the Mastermind and Save the Duke ending in Guarda Fortress Abyss
    LUCK 8 1 Abyss 3: Get the perfect ending for all three Old District (Slum) Faction requests
    LUCK 9 1 Abyss 4: Get all three best endings on Witch Hunt in Elden Hill Village for details
    LUCK 10 1 Abyss 4: Defeat the final version (4th fight) of the GWO

    You start with 4 Mind Pearls. Obtaining all currently available Lamps will give you a total of 47 Mind Pearls. (updated 13 Jan 2026)

    Help, I'm Missing Guiding Lights!

    If you met the requirements to obtain a guiding light but don't have it actually available in the Well for use, try exiting and restarting the game. Some of them seem to need a reboot to show up properly.
